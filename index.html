About NewJeans

NewJeans is a South Korean girl group formed by ADOR, a subsidiary of HYBE Corporation, known for producing global acts like BTS. The group debuted on July 22, 2022, with their self-titled extended play, *New Jeans*. Their name symbolizes timeless fashion, reflecting their desire to create music and styles that remain fresh and relevant over time. They quickly gained attention for their unique approach to K-pop, focusing on a more casual, nostalgic image that contrasts with the flashy concepts often seen in the industry.


The group consists of five members: Minji, Hanni, Danielle, Haerin, and Hyein, who represent a mix of cultures and backgrounds. Their music blends genres like R&B, pop, and retro sounds, appealing to a broad audience.
